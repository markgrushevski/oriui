OriSurface — a minimal elevated floating-surface primitive: surface background + optional hairline + radius + a mode-aware `--ori-shadow-*` elevation, with no padding or content semantics of its own (the caller owns the layout inside). The building block for chrome that floats over content — a toolbar island, a panel, a popout — and the elevation counterpart to OriCard (a content card). Props: `as`, `bordered`, `elevation` (`sm` / `md` / `lg`), `radius`.
